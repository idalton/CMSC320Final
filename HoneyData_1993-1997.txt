Honey:  Final Estimates by State and United States, 1993-97, U.S. Department of
Agriculture, Agricultural Statistics Board, National Agricultural Statistics
Service, Statistical Bulletin No. 956.
 
Introduction: This bulletin presents final estimates, including revisions made
by the Agricultural Statistics Board (ASB), for the production years 1993
through 1997.  Honey producing colonies, yield per colony, honey production,
producer held stocks, price per pound, and value of production were published
in calendar years 1993-1997 in annual ASB reports.  Data included in this
publication provide a historic record and benchmark for current estimates.
 
Procedures: Revisions were made after a thorough review of the original survey
data and all additional data now available, including the 1997 Census of
Agriculture.  The 1993-1997 estimates shown herein are considered final and
will not be revised again.  Revisions to honey produced at the National level
are as follows:
 
                    Pounds of Honey Produced

               1994         217,168       218,187       +0.5
               1995    210,516       211,073       +0.3
               1996    198,197            199,511  +0.7
               1997    192,393            196,536       +2.2
 
Historic Estimates:  Final estimates of Honey Production in earlier years are
available in Statistical Bulletins No. 912 for 1986-92, No. 673 for 1976-79,
No. 614 for 1970-75, No. 500 for 1965-69, and No. 388 for 1955-64.  NASS
estimates for honey were not made for the years 1982-1985.
